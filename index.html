INTERESTS

Kayaking

I joined Warwick Canoe Club in second year Warwick University, and since I have been kayaking across the UK on various rivers. My first trip was to the River Dee when it was a freezing -1°C, but the thrill of paddling through the rapids was unforgettable. Since then I have gone on many trips, with the incredible 10 day trip in Scotland. There I experienced the incredible rapids of the River Gary, and waterfalls of the River Etive. I also got an incredible opportunity to be the Canoe Society's Media Officer, creating Instagram and Facebook content for the society; spreading awareness of the events. It has been a great learning experience for communication, teamwork and leadership; as well as pushing me way beyond my comfort zone. I hope to get up to more in the future!

Travelling

In 2021, I went on an Interrail trip with my closest friends from primary school. We visited 11 countries along the way, first doing France, Spain and Portugal, before meeting back up in the Netherlands and doing Eastern Europe. I adored Barcelona and Porto, for their food and culture. Amsterdam and Berlin had an incredible night life, with remarkable history. Prague had some of the best beer; and Budapest had some incredible bathhouses with amazing food in the Jewish Quarter. Our trip ended in Munich, catching an Ed Sheeran concert in the Olympic Stadium. It was an unforgettable experience, and it sparked a love for travelling.
